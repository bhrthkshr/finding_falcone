Finding Falcone

Welcome to Planet Lengaburu

After the recent Falicornian war, King Shan has exiled Queen Al Falcone for 15 years. However, if he finds her before the 15 years are up, she has to go into exile for another 15 years!

King Shan has received intelligence that Al Falcone is hiding in one of six neighbouring planets. However he has limited resources at his disposal & can send his army to only four of these planets.

  • DonLon
  • Enchai
  • Jebing
  • Sapir
  • Lerbin
  • Pingasor

You need to help King Shan to choose the planets and the vehicles to search for Queen AI Falcone.

Begin Search

Instructions

  • Select the planets you want to search in for AI falcone.
    • All planets at varying distances from Lengaburu.
    • you can Select any four planets out of six planets
  • Drag and Drop the vehicle you want to use to send to the planets you selected.
    • The units of each vehicle type vary.
    • All have different ranges (maximum distance it can travel).
    • If the range for a vehicle is lesser than the distance to the planet, it cannot be chosen for going to the planet.
    • All have different speed.
